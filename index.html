Who Are We?
Achievers Investment and Management LTD is a Ghanaian owned industrial company that aims at revolutionizing the agricultural, impex industries of the country.
With a combined staff experience of more than 200 years we aim to become Africa’s foremost authority on strategic agricultural investments, standardised industrial practice and innovative export and import technologies. Our staff is made up of dynamic, young and self-driven professionals who work tirelessly to maintain the ethos of the company by exhibiting professionalism, integrity, customer service, and effective resource management. We are focussed in three main industries; Agriculture, import and export and industrial training.
